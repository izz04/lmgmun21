Guidelines

| Please read before submitting your application |

  • The minimum number of delegates participating as a school delegation should be 9. The delegates can be from classes 8-12.

  • Schools are requested to send in their confirmation by 15th May, 2020 and complete registration by 30th May, 2020 by filling out the school delegation registration form at https://lmgmun.org/register.html.

  • The LMGMUN Secretariat would be happy to equip each school and individual delegate with all other documents related to the Conference, including accommodation deals at discounted rates after registration.

  • The Conference Handbook, which will include a detailed guide to our Rules of Procedure, Dress Code and a tentative Conference Schedule, will be made available on our website by 30th May, 2020.

  • The Agendas being discussed will be elaborated upon in the Background Guides, which will be at the delegates’ disposal by 10th June, 2020 and would be accessible via our website.

  • The registration fee per delegate is INR 2000.00 and the school delegation registration fee is INR 1500.00 for local/national institutions.

  • We will be happy to welcome all international delegations at the same registration fee as the national delegations. The La Martiniere Girls’ Model United Nations Secretariat shall oblige to provide all assistance required for necessary documentation for obtaining VISAs for the country. The registration fee for the international delegates will be in accordance to the price of the US Dollars in conformity with the Indian Rupee at the time of registration.

Steps to Register

Step 1

Fill out the registration form at www.lmgmun.org/register.html.

Step 2

Make the payment through one of the modes specified in the email sent after registration.

Step 3

Send a copy of the Aadhar and PAN cards of each delegate and accompanying teacher to secretariat@lmgmun.org. Committees and Portfolios shall be allocated once the payment has been made.
