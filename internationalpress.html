INTERNATIONAL PRESS





The Spectator

The International Press of La Martiniere Girls' Model United Nations 2020 is a team dedicated to bringing to you content from around the world with its annual newsletter, "The Spectator", which tackles the likes of misogyny and international crises alike. This organ of the MUN circuit aims to compile global news and present the same with a dash of tasteful humour and imagery.
